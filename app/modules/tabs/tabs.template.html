Vertical

Vertical tabs could not be reproduced 1:1 between Foundation for Sites and ui.bootstrap.

Known issues:

  • The tab headings (the "navigation" of a tabset) stretches to match the tab content

To use vertical tabs, you need to add both of the classes uib-tabset & vertical to the uib-tabset - this is important, and is not required by ui.bootstrap out of the box.
